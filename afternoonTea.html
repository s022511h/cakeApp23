Afternoon Tea



Embark on a refined culinary journey by booking Afternoon Tea at Watson Treats, where every moment is an indulgence in sophistication and flavor. Our extensive tea selection caters to diverse preferences, ranging from classic Earl Grey to exotic fruit infusions. The teas are expertly paired with an array of handcrafted sandwiches, showcasing flavors that range from the timeless cucumber and cream cheese to the more adventurous smoked salmon. The centerpiece of our Afternoon Tea experience is an exquisite assortment of cakes that cater to the most discerning palates. From decadent chocolate creations to delicately layered sponge cakes, each bite is a celebration of culinary artistry. Complementing these delights are warm, freshly baked scones served with generous portions of clotted cream and a selection of homemade jams, adding a touch of tradition to your experience.



At Watson Treats, we elevate the Afternoon Tea experience by serving it on beautiful pottery inspired by the ancient Japanese art of kintsugi. The unique and artful presentation of our treats enhances the overall aesthetic, making your visit not just a meal but a visual feast.
