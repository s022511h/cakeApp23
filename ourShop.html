Our shop

Watson Treats, a beloved family-owned bakery nestled near the vibrant city of Liverpool. With a rich history dating back to 1910, our shop has been a cornerstone of the community, enchanting generations with the aroma of freshly baked delights and the warmth of a true family business.

At Watson Treats, we take pride in crafting not just cakes, but experiences that bring people together. Step into our welcoming shop, where every corner is steeped in the legacy of a century-old tradition. Our commitment to quality and authenticity shines through in every bite, a testament to the passion that has been passed down through the Watson family.

Indulge in the divine sweetness of our signature cakes. The Victoria Sponge, with its light and fluffy layers, captures the essence of timeless elegance. For chocolate lovers, our Chocolate Fudge Cake is a heavenly symphony of rich cocoa and velvety fudge. The Cheesecake, a decadent masterpiece, is a favorite among those seeking a creamy and luscious treat.

But the delight doesn't end there – we invite you to explore a diverse array of confections that cater to every palate. From delightful cupcakes adorned with artistic swirls to our famous marble cake loaves, each creation is a labor of love, made fresh daily for you to savor.

What sets Watson Treats apart is not just the delectable array of cakes but the sense of community we foster. As you walk through our doors, you become a part of our extended family, where every visit is a cherished moment. Join us in celebrating the joy of tradition, the warmth of family, and the irresistible allure of our delightful cakes.


Come, experience the magic of Watson Treats – where every bite tells a story, and every customer is a cherished member of our sweet family.
